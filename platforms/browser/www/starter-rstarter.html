R-Type Starter Motors

SEG AUTOMOTIVE

  • Benefits
  • Tech Specs
  • Designed to serve the entry level segment of commercial applications with 12 V on-board power supply
  • Six-pole motor with strong permanent magnets for generating the electrical field
  • Powerful solution for 3 l diesel engines and smaller (output power up to 2.5 kW (at –20 °C))
  • Low weight, compact design
  • Available as a nose-type starter motor (R78) and nose-less design (RF78) (nose-less is optional variant)
  • Robust starter motor components
  • The R(F)78 starter motors are equipped with an overload protection package as standard.
  • optional equipment features:
    • increased protection (IP) against environmental impacts,
    • thermal protection switch to prevent overheating
  • High cold start performance for good starting capability – achieved with powerful permanent magnets and high torque from transmission ratios
  • Reduced weight – reduces fuel consumption
  • Compact dimensions – require less packaging space
  • Robust design – makes the starter motor resistant to vibrations and protects it from pasting as well as from dirt and water ingress
  • Overload protection – protects the starter motor against overheating, e.g. resulting from misuse

HXF87 Heavy Duty Starter Motors

SEG AUTOMOTIVE

  • Features
  • Benefits
  • Tech Specs
  • Four-pole, electrically excited heavy duty starter motor family for medium output segment
  • Nose-type starter motor version for 12 V and nose-less version for 24 V on-board power supply
  • High power output to crank diesel engines up to 6l displacement (in 12 V) and up to 8.5 l (in 24 V) – with extended range of batteries
  • Electric one-stage pinion engagement
  • Standard at HXF88-M 24 V:
    • Unique inner design – “non-sliding clutch”
    • Integrated mechanical relay (IMR) reliably switches all start functions between starter motor and engine control unit / on-board computer respectively (IMR also available with 2-pin plug connector)
  • life-time of 800.000 road km in on-highway or 14.000 operating hours in off-road use
  • High output for excellent cold start performance
  • Robust design – returning high lifetime; in case of HXF88-M 24V with reduced moving masses to achieve smooth engagement in order to limit wear at starter motor pinion and engine’s ring-gear
  • Small sized product for improved packaging
  • Overload protection
  • Nose-less design version HXF88-M 24V offers higher degree of front-face protection against environmental impacts (dust, water etc.) and also higher flexibility for mounting
  • Range of optional features available for individual adaption and optimized solutions for use-cases
    • especially for nose-type version HX87-M 12V

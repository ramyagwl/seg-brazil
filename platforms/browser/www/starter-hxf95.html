HXF95 Heavy Duty Starter Motors

SEG AUTOMOTIVE

  • Features
  • Benefits
  • Tech Specs
  • Four-pole, electrically excited heavy duty starter motor family for high output segment
  • Nose-type version for 12 V and nose-less version for 24 V on-board power supply
  • High power output to crank diesel engines up to 9l displacement (in 12 V) and up to 13 l (in 24 V)
  • Standard at HXF95-L 24 V:
    • Electric two-stage pinion engagement
    • Integrated mechanical relay (IMR) reliably switches all start functions between starter motor and engine control unit / on-board computer respectively (IMR also available with 2-pin plug connector)
  • life-time of 800.000 road km in on-highway or 14.000 operating hours in off-road use
  • High output for excellent cold start performance
  • Robust design – returning high lifetime
  • Overload protection
  • Nose-less design version HXF95-L 24V offers higher degree of front-face protection against environmental impacts (dust, water etc.) and also higher flexibility for mounting
  • Range of optional features available for individual adaption and optimized solutions for use-cases - especially for nose-type version HX95-L 12V
